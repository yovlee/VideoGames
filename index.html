Intro :

Video games have been around since 1962, when "Spacewar!" was created.

There is a debate whether these virtual games are a good thing or not. Some people argue that they are a great piece of technology and entertainment, however others argue that it is negatively affecting new generations, causing them to act violently or inappropriately. Many video games have been produced since "Spacewar!" Some include the Mario Kart games, in which you race other players ( or CPU's depending on the game mode ) and try to win which ever cup you are racing for.
Or the Super Mario Bros. Games, where you play as Mario ( or any other given characters, like Luigi, Toad, etc. ) to save Princess Peach who constantly gets kidnapped by Bowser.
There is also Super Smash Bros. in which all our favorite characters from different games join together to brawl each other. In this game, more was included. They added spirits and assist trophies.
